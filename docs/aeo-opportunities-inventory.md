## Recommended A/B Test Strategy

### Phase 1: Near-me Meta Description Test (All High Priority Pages)
- **Test**: Geographic/locality modifier in meta descriptions
- **Pages**: Homepage, Our Work, About, Contact
- **Duration**: 4-6 weeks minimum
- **Metrics**: CTR from SERPs, organic traffic, time on page

### Phase 2: Action-Oriented CTA Test
- **Test**: Passive descriptions vs. active CTAs
- **Pages**: Enquiry, Contact, Homepage
- **Duration**: 4 weeks
- **Metrics**: Form submissions, CTR, bounce rate

### Phase 3: Industry-Specific Landing Test
- **Test**: Lead with different industries (mining vs. agriculture)
- **Pages**: Homepage, Our Work
- **Duration**: 6-8 weeks
- **Metrics**: Traffic by keyword, form submissions by industry

---

## Key AEO Principles to Test

1. **Locality Signals**: "near me", "in Germiston", "Gauteng"
2. **Answer-Ready Format**: Direct, concise answers to implied questions
3. **Service Modifiers**: "expert", "certified", "OEM", "fast", "emergency"
4. **Proof Points**: Years in business, combined experience, service areas
5. **Clear CTAs**: Action verbs ("Get", "View", "Contact", "Call")
6. **Industry Specificity**: Lead with target industries and use cases
7. **Trust Signals**: "Certified", "Trusted", "Guaranteed", "Professional"
