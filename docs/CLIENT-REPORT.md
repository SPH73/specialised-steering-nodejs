## What We've Built

### 1. A/B Testing System

**What It Does:**
When someone visits your website, they're randomly shown one of two versions of your page description (the text that appears in Google search results). We track which version leads to more enquiries, then keep the winner.

**How It Works:**
- 50% of visitors see Version A (your current descriptions)
- 50% of visitors see Version B (optimized descriptions)
- After 4-6 weeks, we analyze the results
- We implement the winning version for everyone

**What's Being Tested:**
- **Homepage** - Your main landing page
- **Our Work** - Your portfolio page
- **About** - Your company information page
- **Contact** - Your contact page

**No Changes to Your Website:**
Your website looks and works exactly the same. Only the preview text in Google search results is being tested.

## Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

### What Is AEO?

AEO is the next evolution of SEO. With AI-powered search (ChatGPT, Google's AI Overviews, Bing Copilot), search engines are becoming "answer engines" that directly answer questions.

**Example:**
- **Old search:** User types "hydraulic repairs" ‚Üí sees list of websites
- **New search:** User asks "Who repairs hydraulic cylinders near me?" ‚Üí sees direct answer with your business highlighted

**How We've Optimized for AEO:**
1. **Locality signals** - "Germiston", "Gauteng", "near me"
2. **Direct answers** - Answer implied questions clearly
3. **Industry specificity** - "mining", "agriculture", "trucks"
4. **Trust indicators** - "40+ years", "OEM-certified", "expert"
5. **Action-oriented** - Clear next steps for visitors

## Questions & Answers

### Q: Will this affect my current search rankings?
**A:** No. If anything, rankings will improve as your CTR increases. Google rewards pages that get more clicks.

### Q: What if Version B performs worse?
**A:** We'll detect that within 2-3 weeks and stop the test, reverting to your current descriptions. No harm done.

### Q: Can I see both versions?
**A:** Yes. Clear your cookies and visit your site multiple times. You'll see different versions randomly assigned.

### Q: Will this confuse my customers?
**A:** No. Each customer sees only one consistent version throughout their journey. The actual website content is identical.

### Q: How long before I see results?
**A:** Meaningful data: 2-3 weeks. Statistical significance: 4-6 weeks. Full results: 6-8 weeks.

### Q: What if I want to stop the test?
**A:** Just let us know. We can stop and revert at any time with no penalty.

### Q: Will this affect my Google Ads?
**A:** No. Ads are separate from organic search. This only affects natural search results.

### Q: Can we test other things later?
**A:** Absolutely! This is Phase 1. We have Phase 2 and 3 ready to go if you'd like to continue optimizing.
